About

The family company "Grbo-MNM" started operating back in 2003. We started our trading business with a ceramics and equipment salon "AZZURRO". With quality offer and service, while following the market, we quickly grew into a serious partner to all our customers, investors and construction companies. Today we represent world and European brands that are synonymous with quality and a guarantee of safe and infallible choice.
